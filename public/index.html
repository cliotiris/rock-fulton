About This Page

Hola! Welcome to the Rock Fulton web app! I made this page for the Fulton Rocks Facebook group. I wanted to play with some new technology (Firebase), and this simple app was my side project to do so. As a bonus, maybe you'll get a kick out of finding and placing rocks using it!

The idea is simple. Sign in, and you get the ability to "find" and "place" rocks. No worries, I only store your name and photo, as well as any "find" and "place" events you make using this app. If you happen to be using the app and come across a rock that has alread been found, but not marked so, please "find" it so others don't go on a wild goose chase :)

I wrote this fairly quickly, so it may not be completely bug free. Sorry! You can complain to me at @mathieujonson. I also realize it's not the most beautiful thing on the web. What can I say? I'm a developer, not a designer :) I'm also open to feature requests, to a degree. We'll see how it goes. Enjoy the app!
